Safer browsing tips

Inspect the domain

Attackers often hide fake brand names inside long subdomains or replace letters with similar-looking characters.

Be careful with shortened links

A shortener can hide the final destination, which makes scams harder to spot before clicking.

Never trust urgency alone

“Reset now” or “verify immediately” language is common in phishing campaigns that try to rush a decision.
